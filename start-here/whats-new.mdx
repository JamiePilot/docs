## Highlights

- **PilotScore explainability**: score responses now include contributing signals so teams can act without revealing proprietary weights.
- **Blueprint tier gating GA**: enforce follower eligibility with consistent checks on identity, trading history, and acknowledged risk.
- **Execution-confirm audit trails**: detect mismatches between stated strategy actions and executed trades with webhook events.
- **Integration updates**: new sandbox broker simulators and webhook retry headers for idempotent processing.
