## Contact

- Email: `support@pilot.markets`
- Slack/Teams: request a shared channel during onboarding.
- Escalations: `incident@pilot.markets` for production-impacting issues.

## Office hours

We host implementation and compliance reviews weekly. Bring payload examples, Blueprint drafts, and webhook handler code for fast feedback.

## SLAs

- Platform-down / webhook outage: updates within 30 minutes; mitigations prioritized over RCA.
- Scoring delays: updates hourly until resolved.
- Non-urgent questions: responses within 1 business day.
