description: "Common troubleshooting questions."
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I replay a webhook?">
    Use the `webhook.retry` event or request a replay from support with the event ID and timeframe.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why is a follower blocked after being allowed?">
    Score or tier changes can trigger re-checks. Review recent `score.updated` or `blueprint.tier-changed` events.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I test with simulated trades?">
    Yes in sandbox; simulated data is excluded from production scoring and suitability.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
