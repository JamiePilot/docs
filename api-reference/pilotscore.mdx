---
title: "PilotScore"
description: "Endpoints to retrieve scores and histories."
---

## Get score

`GET /pilot-score/{navigatorId}` or `GET /navigators/{id}/pilot-score`

## Get history

`GET /pilot-score/{navigatorId}/history?limit=50`

Includes timestamps, deltas, and reasons per change.

## Webhooks

Subscribe to `score.updated` for changes without polling.
