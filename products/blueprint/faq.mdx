---
title: "Blueprint FAQ"
description: "Answers to common questions about tiering, edits, and reviews."
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do we handle edits to a live Blueprint?">
    Edits create a new version. Followers can adopt it; eligibility and guardrails re-evaluate automatically.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Who assigns tiers?">
    Co-Pylot reviewers map declared risk to the tier matrix, with overrides logged and time-bound.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What triggers re-review?">
    Material changes to leverage, instruments, locks, or repeated execution-confirm mismatches.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can a Blueprint be unpublished?">
    Yes. Unpublishing halts new followers; existing followers are notified and can unwind per your policy.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can traders see my exact rules?">
    You control visibility: public summary vs full detail for followers.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to be a Navigator to author?">
    Any trader can author a Blueprint; Navigator programs add discovery perks and copy caps.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
