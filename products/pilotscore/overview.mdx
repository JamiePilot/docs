## What it measures

PilotScore is a transparent, on-chain credibility rating that rewards discipline, consistency, and community impact. It looks at verified trading telemetry, adherence to Blueprint rules, and how followers fare when they mirror you.

## What it's used for

- Ranking Navigators and Blueprint authors in discovery experiences.
- Unlocking higher copy-trade caps and front-page placement for consistent traders.
- Gating followers and escalating reviews when behavior drifts.
- Providing explainable, verifiable proof to platforms, prop firms, and regulators.
