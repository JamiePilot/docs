---
title: "Co-Pylot FAQ"
description: "Common admin and workflow questions."
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Who can approve a Blueprint?">
    Compliance Reviewers and Admins can approve; approvals are logged with timestamps and reason codes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What's logged?">
    Every suitability decision, override, export, and webhook delivery attempt is captured in the audit log.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do we export evidence?">
    Use exports for audit-ready bundles or API access under `/audit/events` with filters for date, Navigator, or follower.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can we customize alerts?">
    Yes-configure thresholds per event type and route to teams or tools (email, webhook, incident systems).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can followers pause copy?">
    Yes-followers can pause or cap risk anytime; events log the change and resume state.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do guardrails carry over when copying?">
    Yes-auto sizing, locks, and stops from the Blueprint stay enforced in Co-Pylot unless an approved override exists.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
