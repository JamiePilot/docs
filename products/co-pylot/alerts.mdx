## Events

- `score.updated`: PilotScore changes beyond configured thresholds.
- `blueprint.rule-violated`: guardrail hit during copy trading.
- `execution.mismatch`: execution-confirm detected a deviation.
- `copy.latency`: fills exceeded latency/slippage thresholds.
- `webhook.retry`: delivery retries after non-2xx responses.

## Routing

- Ops: guardrail triggers, latency spikes, follower caps reached.
- Compliance: rule violations, overrides expiring, repeated mismatches.
- Support: follower questions, disclosure updates, and dispute flags.

## Playbooks

- Auto-pause copy when hard locks fire; resume only after acknowledgement.
- Request verification when score bands fall or latency remains high.
- Notify Navigators when drift or slippage approaches thresholds.
