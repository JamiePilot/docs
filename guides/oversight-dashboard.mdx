## Metrics to monitor

- Distribution of PilotScore by segment and tier
- Blueprint tier mix vs follower profiles
- Execution-confirm mismatch rate and remediation time
- Suitability decision outcomes (allow/warn/block) over time
- Top deltas: Navigators with biggest score changes

## Usage

Use dashboards to spot drift, identify outliers, and sample decisions with explanations. Pair with [Audit logs](/security/audit-logs) for evidence.
