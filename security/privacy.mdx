## Data inventory

| Data | Purpose | Retention |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Profile metadata | Identify Navigators/followers | Lifecycle of account |
| Trade history | Scoring, execution-confirm | Contracted retention; purge on request when permitted |
| Suitability decisions | Evidence for regulators | Minimum 7 years (configurable) |
| Webhook payloads | Delivery + replay for retries | 14 days |

## Requests

- Data access and deletion requests: `privacy@pilot.markets`
- Configurable retention windows available per environment.

<Note>
Suitability decisions often require long retention; coordinate with your counsel before shortening windows.
</Note>
