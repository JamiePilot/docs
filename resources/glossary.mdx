---
title: "Glossary"
description: "Definitions for Pilot terms."
---

**Audit log** - Record of suitability-affecting actions, exports, and webhook deliveries.

**Blueprint** - Structured strategy definition mapped to a tier for suitability.

**Execution-confirm** - Mechanism to validate executed trades against declared actions.

**Navigator** - Strategy owner who publishes Blueprints.

**PilotScore** - Compliance-grade trust protocol rating trading behavior.

**Suitability check** - Decision using follower profile, PilotScore, and Blueprint tier.

**Tier** - Classification of strategy complexity and risk used to gate followers.
